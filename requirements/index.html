  • A-spotOpen or Close
    Homepage
    Mr. Robot
    Show Landing Page

    Goal

    Give special treatment to promotional content and gain traction.

    Current features

    2 versions of an A-spot module: They can’t say exactly the same thing thus has to be contextual.
    - Homepage: Allows to isolate the piece of the image. May move away from this.
    - Show: Has the right-side module.

    Predetermined font sizes: 6 sizes, based on the length of the text.

    Views for smaller devices: Small phone vs. everything else. The latter proportionally scales, fixed dimension.

    Must-haves

    Precise control over the presentation: Currently, the producers and designers have control over various presentational aspects, from text sizes and placements to the cropping of the peek. They want to precisely control how the images and texts would be displayed in screens of multiple sizes.

    Ability to:
    - Drag and drop text and move it around
    - Breakpoint control, responsive
    - Have multiple CTAs

    Pain Points

    From Tech Lead

    Asset management: Wants to reuse assets wherever possible.

    No real-time previews: Previews don’t appear until the A-spot is saved as draft.

    Concern

    1 template for all the shows won’t work due to the text length and sizes.

    Other

    Used to burn the images, which was annoying.

  • PreviewOpen or Close

    Goal

    Proofread and check the content and images to get accurate and beautiful pages, and share the vision both internally and externally.

    Current features

    Preview Homepage: Separate nodes and previews by day

    Preview pages and content type
    - Homepage
    - Show landing page, especially Best Of section
    - Episode Guide, Post, Gallery, Person, Quiz, Timeline, A-spot

    No preview feature at all on OTT apps.

    Must-haves

    Homepage: Knowing which day you are editing and viewing

    Preview of future state: Pick a future date to preview, preferably in the past as well

    Shareability: Executives and sponsors to be able to review and give feedback, ideally without logging in

    Mobile preview: for editors and designers

    Use Cases

    - Homepage, Show landing pages

    - Update every week, 6am: full episode, recap, gallery

    Pain Points

    From Tech Lead

    When adding a new show, there are 60~70 key artworks to check for.

    Troubleshooting can be frustrating with multiple assets.

    From Producer

    Currently, a designer has to mock up the page in order to share the future state with executives, which takes few hours of the designer's time.

    Producers can’t preview the content that is in an unpublished state.

    Concern

    There could be an security issue if the preview was generated to be shared with external parties without logins.

    Other

    If cache bust is available, the preview might be less necessary.

    Low-fidelity preview is not necessary, as the main purpose of the preview is to view how it would actually look on the front-end site. It would be only necessary for content proofing, but USA doesn’t have that workflow as a small team.

  • ScheduleOpen or Close

    Goal

    Other

    USA doesn’t really need an end date, as they rarely unpublish content.

    Videos have sunrise and sunset dates.

  • QuizzesOpen or Close
    Mr. Robot
    Season 1 Trivia

    Goal

    Provide interactive experience to the viewers, and gain traction via social sharing.

    Current Features

    - Profiler: Answer the questions to find out what character you are.

  • Live TVOpen or Close
    Live TV Page

    Goal

    Offer live TV program and related content while the show is playing live.

    Current Features

    Live progamming: Serve live content based on what’s playing right now.

    Related content: Can dynamically serve gallery (recap), quiz.

  • Series, Season, Episode GuideOpen or Close
    Colony
    Show Landing Page
    Suits
    Episode Guide

    Goal

    Promote and display content related to a show and its episodes.

    Current Features

    Promotions on the landing page: A-spot, Best Of, full bleed promo, latest content, latest episode right rail, etc.

    Menu configuration: Episodes, Videos, Photos, Cast & Info, Blog, link to microsites, etc.

    Weekly update of contents: Full episode, clips, galleries, episode recaps, blog posts, etc.

    Pain Points

    There is no dedicated template for shows in the pre-air state with small amount of contents. A temporary redirect is set to the Cast & Info page, which holds about text, series art, and any cast information or trailer available at the time.

    The full site template on the front-end is inflexible and doesn't adapt to the amount of contents on the page. Latest Full Episodes right rail would appear as an empty black box without any episode or alternate content assigned to it, and Best Of section needs all 5 items to fill in all the blocks.

    New Design

    Clear hierarchy & relationship: Series, Season, Episode nodes are created and the relationship among the nodes are specified through Channel Reference field.

    Episode node for all episode-related content: Episode node holds all information related to the specific episode. The Long Description field of the node holds the Episode Recap, and any full episode and clips related to the episode will be associated in the Media card of the Episode.

  • Promo & Full-bleed PromoOpen or Close
    Homepage
    Full-bleed Promo

    Goal

    Effectively promote the content that would interest the users the most and/or sponsored.

    Current Features

    Separate Content Types: A-spot, Full Bleed Promo, and generic Promo content types for different usage and different display options

    Common elements: Promotional text, images for different platforms, call-to-action links

    New Design

    Multi-purpose Promo Content Type: Promo content type has all the common elements of the current promotional content types. The look of the Promo items would be determined by the front-end application based on the context and placement of the Promo.

  • Media GalleryOpen or Close
    Falling Water
    Media Gallery

    Goal

    Sequentially display images and captions related to a show or a movie.

    Current Features

    Basic gallery features: Image, caption, association to series, season, & episode, gallery type (Episodic, Character, GIF, other), etc.

    Customizaton of end card: Set URL to go to when the last item of the gallery is clicked

    Social sharing: Ability to share the entire gallery or specific photo of the gallery to various social media

    Must-have

    Ability to add both images and videos

    Multiple associations to shows and characters

    Pain Points

    Setting focal point is tricky with the small crosshair and the cursor covering over it.

    No feedback or progress bar while uploading images.

    Dragging around the images to rearrange is slow.

    Negative numbers on row weights are unintuitive.

    Season and Episode dropdowns are populated too slow.

    New Design

    Full Gallery Features: Image, captions that are specific to the gallery, channel references, and Category to cover all the basic gallery features

    Easy Rearranging: Supports both drag & drop and number ordering. Grid view makes it easier and faster to drag the assets around, and numbers are automatically set to positive numbers.

  • PostOpen or Close
    Eyewitness
    Post

    Goal

    Provide the visitors more in-depth information of the show or related contents using text and various media.

    Current Features

    Full Rich Text Editor capability: Basic text formatting, including heading, paragraph, bold, italicize, hyperlinks, etc. Producers can also embed various assets, such as images, MPX videos, social media, external videos, etc.

    Social Sharing: Ability to share the specific blog post to various social platforms

    Pain Points

    Producers need to insert raw HTML code in the post in order to embed external assets or design the assets.

    Associating a wrong show in the Show dropdown affects Omniture reporting, and modifying the show later doesn't get reflected.

    Must-haves

    High-priority feature

    Will have listicle as a separate widget

    New Design

    Full Rich Text Editor Capability: All the rich text editor capabilities in the current CMS are preserved.

    Listicle: Separate Listicle asset in the new design can be embedded anywhere in the Post's text and stylized by the front-end application.

    Enhanced UI and Workflow: Embedding various assets are easier with the embed options available upfront and fields specific to the asset type.

  • Actor & Character BiosOpen or Close
    Queen of the South
    Character Bio

    Goal

    Provide biographical information for both a character and an actor who plays the role in the show.

    Current Features

    Basic Cast Features: Name, image, bio text with full rich text editor capability, association to a series, etc.

    Social Sharing: Ability to share each character/actor bio page

    New Design

    Separate Person and Role Content Types: Separate content types for real Person and fictional Role make each node reusable in cases where the actor performs in another show in the same network. This also allows an actor to have multiple roles and vice versa.

    Season-level association: The association between the Person and Role are made in the Season node to accomodate cases where there are different arts or different set of casts per season.
